First-Year Halloween Pickleball — 64-Team Bracket

Four regions of 16 teams each → Final Four → Champion

Click a team to advance → Winners auto-propagate 64 teams • 4×(Round of 16 → Regional Champ)
How to use
  1. Click Create 64 Team Inputs. Enter 16 team names for each region (or use 🎲 to fill samples).
  2. Click Generate Bracket. Click a team in any match to advance it.
  3. Regional champions feed the Final Four (semi-1: NW vs SW, semi-2: NE vs SE), then Championship.
  4. Use Print / Save PDF or share the URL to show the bracket state on any device.
